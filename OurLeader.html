Our Leader

  • Our leader is an intensely private man.
  • Pressure from those who do not understand his message have forced him to tread cautiously.
  • Only the first disciples, and a select few of our flock have met him.
  • It is said his smile warms the soul, and his laugh echoes like thunder!
  • It is said when the end times comes, he will emerge from hiding and lead us to our destiny!
  • For now we wait, and carry on his message and mission to unite the world!
